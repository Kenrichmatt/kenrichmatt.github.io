Description


Established in 2016,we now are working on soccer training for youth player. We build up in a town of ZunYi City,GuiZhou Province,China in 2016.We now developing Soccer,Basketball,Table Tennis and Badminton after three years of deposition.Our company is located in Worker’s Palace of Culture of ZunYi, which is an important place to attract and organize employees to engage cultural activitie and it is a garden for employees and their families.

In 2018,we’ve made an important strategic direction adjustment and signed a series of important agreements with primary schools around XinPu New District,aiming to develop the local football level better. In January 2019, we led our team to the Qian Tai Feng Cup in Thailand and won the first place in the Chinese team. This step represents that we may seek more cooperation in the world and lead our team to play world-class matches in the future,as many as possible,such as Gothic Cup,Mediterranean Intenational Cup(MIC),etc.

At the same time, this year we will sign an important strategic cooperation agreement with the Southwest Branch of CFC(China Football College), which will provide better transportation channels for our players.The signing of the agreement means that we have absolutely excellent resources in the local area. We hardly need to go out to recruit players. We can recruit them well in schools that we cooperated through corresponding experience classes and community classes.What need to be noted is that we maintain a good cooperative relationship with the local football association. The local football association always giving us the strongest support and we will enjoy a series of policies that are very beneficial to us at usual,which will enable our club to develop in the local area without any obstacles.

The last but not least that we have the best hardware facilities in the city. There are many hardware facilities,including,but not limited to，Football pitch around Worker’s Palace of Culture,Southwest Branch of CFC and Olympic Center. We possess Gymnasium,Yoga Room even swimming pool that we can use it for free.

Every foreign coach who joins us will enjoy the same welfare as the local coach provided by our company.

Roles & Responsibilities


The Soccer Head Coach(Men’s) position requires demonstrated ability to interacts directly with coaches,parents,and student-athletes as a representative of Boer Sports.The Head Coach oversees a school athletic team and provides leadership in the development and evaluation of the team(include coach team) and individual student-athletes. At the same time, the Head Coach also requires ability to apply rigorous coach skill, to work harmoniously in a team, and to exercise good judgment. In addition, the Head Coach provides dynamic, strategic and visionary leadership designed to enhance student and coach team success through the focus on club’s Agenda. The level and quality of these skills will be a determining factor in the selection for the position, and references will be given consideration in this determination.

The Soccer Head Coach will work under supervision of the Youth Training Director(YTD) and Executive Director(ET) and day-to-day guidance will be provided by a YTD or ET in the field. The Head Coach will be expected to:

▶ Implements the District wide Athletic Department strategy focused on club’s Agenda designed to increase student-athlete success and improve the success rate of transportate to another professional clubs,schools.Assists the YTD in monitoring student-athlete skill progress. Ensures that athletes reach their main goals by monitoring each age groups attendance and ensuring that they can receive supports as necessary in each semester/season;

▶ Assists players in reaching highest athletic potential possible through athletic training, leadership skills and method of training. Counsels and advises students with respect to athletic performance and other personal development issues. Prepares the student athletes for competition through planning and conducting well organized practices and contests. Directs the identification and recruitment of new players to the program, including traveling to regional school and club soccer events and showcases;

▶ Work closely with the Athletic Director and Dean of Students,Principal, and other school administrators, faculty and staff to assure student-athletes are academically eligible for a given sport;

▶ Plans and directs the training of student athletes, and assesses individuals’ abilities and skill level to determine appropriate team position. Conducts in-season and off-season team practice and conditioning programs including football techniques,skills training, and tactics and conditioning training;

Qualifications

We’ve made a Selection Criteria for every candidates, the candidate shall possess characteristics that reflect:

Minimum Qualifications:

▶ A bachelor’s degree or equivalent;
▶ Current above UEFA C(include),AFC C(include) certification or equivalent level of certification in another country or continent;
▶ Knowledge of all aspects of the skills involved with this sport;
▶ Previous coaching experience in this sport at the school or club level is desirable but not necessary with all the necessary qualifications;
▶ modify one’s way of coaching to suit the special requirements of each class, offer an individualized quality education for every athletes
▶ A teacher with a current teaching license is preferred but not a requirement;
▶ Patience and affections,love kids;
▶ At least two years of relevant work experience;
▶ Good communications skills;

The Ideal Candidates Shall Possess:

▶ Current above UEFA B(include),AFC B(include) certification or equivalent level of certification in another country or continent;
▶ A scout with the FA Level 2 of Talent Identification or equivalent level of certification in another country or continent;
▶ Three to five years of Soccer Head Coach and relevant work experience;
▶ Excellent research in relevent field, vedio analysis and football data analysis;
▶ Excellent communications skills and interpersonal skills;
▶ Knowledge of the style of modern football and excellent coach practice, as well as familiarity with the Europe’s biggest leagues and Knowledge of current Youth Training System;
▶ Fully proficient in basic computer skills, particularly in using Word, Excel and PowerPoint, internet services;
▶ Ability to work well as a team member in a multi-cultural and multi-ethnic environment;
▶ Ability to work under time pressure and to juggle multiple tasks within tight deadlines;
▶ Retired professional athletes, Goalkeeper coach with certification;
