Description
Established in 2016,we now are working on soccer training for youth player. We build up in a town of ZunYi City,GuiZhou Province,China in 2016.We now developing Soccer,Basketball,Table Tennis and Badminton after three years of deposition.Our company is located in Worker’s Palace of Culture of ZunYi, which is an important place to attract and organize employees to engage cultural activitie and it is a garden for employees and their families.

In 2018,we’ve made an important strategic direction adjustment and signed a series of important agreements with primary schools around XinPu New District,aiming to develop the local football level better. In January 2019, we led our team to the Qian Tai Feng Cup in Thailand and won the first place in the Chinese team. This step represents that we may seek more cooperation in the world and lead our team to play world-class matches in the future,as many as possible,such as Gothic Cup,Mediterranean Intenational Cup(MIC),etc.

At the same time, this year we will sign an important strategic cooperation agreement with the Southwest Branch of CFC(China Football College), which will provide better transportation channels for our players.The signing of the agreement means that we have absolutely excellent resources in the local area. We hardly need to go out to recruit players. We can recruit them well in schools that we cooperated through corresponding experience classes and community classes.What need to be noted is that we maintain a good cooperative relationship with the local football association. The local football association always giving us the strongest support and we will enjoy a series of policies that are very beneficial to us at usual,which will enable our club to develop in the local area without any obstacles.

The last but not least that we have the best hardware facilities in the city. There are many hardware facilities,including,but not limited to，Football pitch around Worker’s Palace of Culture,Southwest Branch of CFC and Olympic Center. We possess Gymnasium,Yoga Room even swimming pool that we can use it for free.

Every foreign coach who joins us will enjoy the same welfare as the local coach provided by our company.

Roles & Responsibilities:
The Soccer Head Coach(Men’s) position requires demonstrated ability to interacts directly with coaches,parents,and student-athletes as a representative of Boer Sports.The Head Coach oversees a school athletic team and provides leadership in the development and evaluation of the team(include coach team) and individual student-athletes. At the same time, the Head Coach also requires ability to apply rigorous coach skill, to work harmoniously in a team, and to exercise good judgment. In addition, the Head Coach provides dynamic, strategic and visionary leadership designed to enhance student and coach team success through the focus on Club’s Agenda. The level and quality of these skills will be a determining factor in the selection for the position, and references will be given consideration in this determination.
